The all Fathers Lasagna

   The All-Father is known for his love of Hearty and filling foods.
        His Love for Lasagna is known throughout the 9 realms
        This is the Recipe he has loved since his last trip to Midgard

    Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • 3 Cans of San Marzano Tomatoes
  • 2 pounds of ground/minced beef
  • Mozzarela cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Fresh Parmesan cheese
  • Bechemel sauce
    • Flour
    • Milk
    • Butter

      • Spices

    • 2 tbps Salt
    • 2 tbps Pepper
    • 1 tbps Rosemary
    • 2 tbps Thyme
    • 3 tbps Basil
    • 5 cloves of Garlic
    • 3-5 Medium Yellow Onions
    • Optional: 5 tbps Herb de Provence can be used in place of Basil, Thyme and Rosemary

    The procedure

    In order to
  1. Prepare the onions and garlic by dicing them roughly.
  2. Seperate half of the onions and garlic. half will be used for the sauce, the other half will be used in the beef
  3. Saute the garlic and onions in a large pot with olive oil until they soften and are fragrant
  4. Add the canned tomatoes to the pot and bring this to a low simmer and season to taste
  5. Simmer the tomato sauce for at least an hour
  6. In a high walled pan, saute the onions and garlic and then brown the minced beef
  7. Once the ground beef has been cooked through, add it to the sauce mixture
  8. Clean the high walled pan, we will use this to make the Bechemel sauce
  9. Heat 2tbsp of butter in the pan, after the butter has browned mix 2 tbsp of flour in the pan to form a roux
  10. Cook the roux until it is a dark blond and then add 2 cups of cold milk to the pan slowly and mix it well until it thickens
  11. You now have a basic Bechemel sauce. you can add 1/2 a cup of the ricotta and mozzarella cheeses. add salt and pepper to taste
  12. Cook the Lasagna noodles per their package instructions and set them aside until they are cooled enough to handle
  13. Once the pasta has cooled a bit and the suace is done, Its time to layer the lasagna.
  14. At the bottom of a baking pan or casserole dish lay down a thin layer of the tomato sauce
  15. On top of the first tomato sauce layer,then pasta,then the riccotta cheese, then the tomato sauce, then the Bechemel sauce, then we loop back to the pasta
  16. The last pasta layer should be topped with the tomato sauce then topped with shredded mozzarella or the freshly grated Parmesan
  17. After the beast has been assembled, bake at 375F for 30 minutes
